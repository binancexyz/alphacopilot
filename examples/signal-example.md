# Example: /signal DOGE

**Signal: DOGE**

**Quick Verdict**
DOGE may have a monitor-worthy setup, but the signal needs confirmation before conviction improves.

**Signal Quality**
Medium — a usable early signal, but still fragile.

**Top Risks**
- A visible signal does not guarantee continuation.
- Weak follow-through can turn momentum into noise.

**Why It Matters**
Signal tools are most useful when they explain signal quality and invalidation conditions instead of just flagging movement.

**What To Watch Next**
- volume confirmation
- supporting liquidity context
- whether attention persists into the next cycle
