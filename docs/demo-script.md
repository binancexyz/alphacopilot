# Demo Script

## Opening
Crypto research is fragmented. Users jump across too many tools just to understand whether a token, wallet, or signal is actually worth attention.

**Binance Alpha Copilot** solves that by combining Binance Skills Hub tools into a signal-and-risk research copilot.

## Demo 1 — `/token PEPE`
Show:
- Quick Verdict
- Signal Quality
- Top Risks
- What To Watch Next

Narration:
This is not just a token summary. It interprets whether the signal deserves attention and what could weaken it.

## Demo 2 — `/wallet 0x...`
Show:
- concentration
- behavior pattern
- risk posture

Narration:
The assistant interprets wallet behavior, not just balances.

## Demo 3 — `/watchtoday`
Show:
- top 3 narratives
- strongest signals
- main risk zones

Narration:
This filters market noise into a daily research brief.

## Demo 4 — `/signal DOGE`
Show:
- signal status
- quality
- fragility
- confirmation conditions

Narration:
The assistant explains whether a signal is meaningful, fragile, or worth waiting on.

## Closing
Most crypto tools show movement.

**Binance Alpha Copilot** helps users decide whether that movement is actually worth conviction.
