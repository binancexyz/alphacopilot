# Terminology

## Signal Quality
A qualitative assessment of whether a setup looks meaningful enough to monitor.

## Conviction
A qualitative summary of how much confidence the assistant has after balancing signal and risk.

## Risk Tags
Compact labels that surface the most important risk categories at a glance.

## Runtime
The system that orchestrates the assistant and tool usage. In this project, the preferred runtime is OpenClaw.

## Normalization
Turning raw tool output into a stable internal shape before analysis.
