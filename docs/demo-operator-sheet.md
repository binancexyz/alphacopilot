# Demo Operator Sheet

## Goal
Run the best short live demo with clear narration and safe fallback lines.

---

## Demo order

### 1. `/price BNB`
**What to say:**
"I’ll start with grounding. This is the compact market truth layer."

**What it proves:**
- clean market card
- fast output
- product-like UX

**Fallback if thin:**
"Even at minimum, this shows the product starts from a compact market truth instead of a wall of text."

---

### 2. `/brief BTC`
**What to say:**
"This is the fast synthesis layer. It compresses context instead of dumping fields."

**What it proves:**
- minimalism
- synthesis quality
- chat-native design

**Fallback if thin:**
"The point here is speed and compression, not depth."

---

### 3. `/audit BNB`
**What to say:**
"This is where Bibipilot becomes security-first instead of hype-first."

**What it proves:**
- first-class audit surface
- visible risk handling
- product judgment

**Fallback if thin:**
"Even when the audit is partial, the product treats that uncertainty explicitly instead of hiding it."

---

### 4. `/signal BNB`
**What to say:**
"A useful product should distinguish between a strong setup, a weak setup, and no matched evidence."

**What it proves:**
- signal logic
- honesty under thin data
- not a hype bot

**Fallback if no signal match:**
"This is still valuable because it shows the product does not invent conviction when live evidence is weak."

---

### 5. `/wallet <address>`
**What to say:**
"Wallet tools often stop at balances. Bibipilot tries to answer whether the wallet is even worth following."

**What it proves:**
- interpretation layer
- follow verdicts
- opinionated design

**Fallback if sparse:**
"The live wallet path is still thinner than token/signal, but the judgment shape is already in place."

---

### 6. `/watchtoday`
**What to say:**
"This is where Bibipilot becomes a daily market board instead of a single-asset tool."

**What it proves:**
- market prioritization
- structured lanes
- live rank/signal integration

**Fallback if some lanes are sparse:**
"The lane structure is already real, and richer live coverage improves what fills those lanes over time."

---

### 7. Show Binance Square proof
**What to say:**
"The loop matters: Bibipilot is not only for internal analysis. It can turn judgment into real Binance Square output."

**What it proves:**
- product loop
- real publishing
- not just chat analysis

---

## Opening line
"Bibipilot is a Binance-native copilot that checks what’s moving, filters what’s dangerous, and publishes what’s worth attention."

## Closing line
"Bibipilot turns noisy crypto signals into clearer judgment, then turns that judgment into publishable Binance Square output."
