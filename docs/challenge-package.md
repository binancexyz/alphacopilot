# Bibipilot Challenge Package

## One-line positioning

**Bibipilot checks what’s moving, filters what’s dangerous, and publishes what’s worth attention.**

---

## Short winning pitch

Bibipilot is a Binance-native research copilot built for a simple problem:

**crypto attention moves faster than clean judgment.**

Traders and creators can see noise everywhere:
- token hype
- smart-money signals
- wallet activity
- narrative rotation
- endless posts

But seeing activity is not the same as knowing:
- what is real
- what is risky
- what is late
- what is worth publishing

Bibipilot closes that gap.

It uses Binance Skills as the evidence layer, then adds a judgment layer on top:
- `/price` for compact market truth
- `/brief` for fast synthesis
- `/risk` and `/audit` for safety-first clarity
- `/token` and `/signal` for conviction and timing
- `/wallet` for follow-or-ignore judgment
- `/watchtoday` for daily market prioritization
- `/meme` for fast meme-style scans

Then it closes the loop by publishing Binance Square posts directly.

So Bibipilot is not just an AI that summarizes crypto.
It is a **research -> judgment -> publishing** pipeline built around Binance workflows.

That means the product is trying to answer:
- should I care?

That makes the story stronger than a generic LLM wrapper.

That makes the product feel complete.

## Judge-facing product story

### Problem
Crypto users face too much fragmented attention and too little judgment.
Signals, token screens, narratives, and wallet activity are easy to surface but hard to trust.

### Solution
Bibipilot turns Binance Skills outputs into cleaner judgment and then turns that judgment into publishable Square content.

### Why now
People do not need more raw crypto text.
They need a system that can:
- filter noise
- add caution
- rank attention
- publish useful insight fast

### Why Bibipilot is different
Bibipilot is not trying to be a full trader.
It is the layer between:
- raw market signals
and
- usable conviction

That is a much sharper role.

---

## Core architecture story

### Step 1 — Research
Bibipilot pulls signal, token, audit, wallet, and market-rank context.

### Step 2 — Judgment
It converts raw outputs into:
- safe vs blocked
- early vs aging vs stale
- concentrated vs cleaner
- track vs unknown vs don't follow
- worth attention vs not worth attention

### Step 3 — Publish
It formats results for chat and Binance Square.

This is the strongest simple description:

**Binance Skills provide evidence. Bibipilot provides judgment. Binance Square becomes the publishing layer.**
